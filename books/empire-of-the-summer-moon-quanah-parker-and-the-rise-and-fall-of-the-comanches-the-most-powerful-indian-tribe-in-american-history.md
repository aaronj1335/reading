I think the account of Cynthia Ann Parker's capture and then attempted re-assimilation into white culture is maybe the closest thing we've got to the idea of time traveling back to an ancient culture.

Age is certainly a factor, but it's hard to miss the fact that she managed to assimilate very well into the more primitive culture, but not at all back into white culture. What does that say about human progress?
