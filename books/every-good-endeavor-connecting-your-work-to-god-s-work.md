I found the concept of "the ministry of competence" incredibly validating.

I also loved the summary of Leaf by Niggle by Tolkien. The idea that, in the vocation God gives us on Earth, we're working to something we'll never really attain, but that it can be attained in eternity, and that endows value and validity to the imperfect work we do here on Earth. That gives me more purpose in what I do.
